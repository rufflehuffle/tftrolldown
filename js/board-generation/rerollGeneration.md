# Reroll Generation

## 2 cost RR

### Budget
- Level 5, 100 gold before buying units and XP
- Buy units
- Buy XP until Lv. 5 34/36 or until 50 gold

### Curve

Stage: Level of Natural Shop
===
1-1: 2
1-2: 3
1-3: 3

2-1: 3
2-2: 4
2-3: 4
2-5: 4
2-6: 5
2-7: 5

3-1: 5
3-2: 5

### Preseeded units
Give 2 copies of the carry and 1 copy of each 2 cost

### Comp detection
Given a final board, detect the carries and tanks
- Main carry: 2 cost carry with the most traits active
- Duo carry: 2 cost carry with the second most traits active
- Tank: 2 cost tank with the most traits active, if tied consider a duo tank
